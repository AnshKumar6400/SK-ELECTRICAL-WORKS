Full Service Capability

Every Electrical Need, Handled

Certified electricians handling the complete spectrum — from diagnostics to commissioning, for homes, offices, and heavy industries across Hyderabad.

  • Lighting Systems

    Indoor and outdoor lighting design, installation, and automation — LED retrofits, street lights, flood lights, and smart controls.

  • AC & HVAC Wiring

    Dedicated circuits, copper wiring, and proper earthing for air conditioning units, VRF systems, and HVAC equipment.

  • Solar Panel Wiring

    Grid-tie and off-grid solar system wiring, inverter connections, net metering setup, and commissioning for rooftop solar installations.

  • Generator & DG Set

    Diesel generator wiring, ATS panel installation, load synchronisation, and AMF panel setup for uninterrupted power backup.

  • Cable Tray & Trunking

    Structured cable management using GI cable trays, HDPE conduits, and PVC trunking for clean, organised, and accessible installations.

  • Earthing & Lightning Protection

    Plate earthing, pipe earthing, chemical earthing, and lightning arrestor installation to protect equipment and personnel from electrical hazards.

  • Fault Detection & Testing

    Thermal imaging, insulation resistance testing, earth loop testing, and phase sequence checks to find and fix hidden electrical faults before they escalate.

  • Chipping & Conduit Routing

    Precision wall chipping, groove cutting, and concealed conduit laying for seamless, hidden wiring routes in new builds and renovations.

  • AMC & Maintenance

    Annual Maintenance Contracts for industrial plants, commercial buildings, and residential complexes — scheduled inspections, testing, and preventive maintenance.
